Hi. I am Jitkapat. I'm a research student (M.Eng) at School of Information Science and Technology, VISTEC. I have a background in humanities, especially literary studies, and a little in introductory linguistics. I am currently supervised by Assoc. Prof. Sarana Nutanong. I am also formerly a part of NLP@Chula group at Faculty of Arts, Chulalongkorn University. Ideally, I am interested in applying NLP and generalized learning algorithms to interdisciplinary studies despite limited resources.
